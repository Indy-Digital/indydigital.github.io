About

Indy Digital provides digital marketing services to partners around the world.

The Team

We are a team built of nerds. We live and breathe our jobs because we love doing what we do. Driving results for our partners is our primary motivation. We build real relationships with our partners. Want to grab drinks after work? Let's do it!

The Company

Indy Digital was founded to combine the perks of using an agency with the perks of having an in-house team. This is why our model is built on transparency and trust. We don't pretend to be magicians or miracle workers, we tell you how it is and work hard every day to get you to your goals.

The Philosophy

We always view ourselves as partners, not vendors. Any data we get is shared with you and we will even go over the learnings and our processes to help you get a better understanding of your investment. We will always tell you the truth even if it is not what you want to hear. We strongly believe that the best thing for any business is kindness and empathy, that is what our foundation was built on.

The Values
