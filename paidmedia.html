Paid Media

We offer a wide range of paid media solutions. Paid media includes any form of paid digital advertising. Such as Google Search Ads, Facebook Ads, YouTube Ads, TikTok Ads, Twitter Ads, Google Display Ads, etc. Any form of content that you pay to get in front of your customers.

Are we qualified?

Our team members hold over 10 paid media certifications on average. Combined we have over 50 years of expereince in the paid media field and have managed over $1,000,000,000 worth of paid digital advertising. Not only do we have the history to back it up but we have in-house requirements on continuing education to make sure we are always up to date on the latest best practices.

Google

The best place to start when looking to get into paid media. Google has sold advertising on their sites for a long time by proving value to advertisers over and over again. As a proven platform for advertising it also provides customization unlike many others. Want to reach customers in market? Google Search. Want to create awareness and reach new customers? Google Display. Want to reach customers who purchased in the past or have shown interest? Google Retargeting.
