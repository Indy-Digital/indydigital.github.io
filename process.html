Process

Here you can get a good understanding of our process and what to expect after hiring us. Take a look around. Keep in mind this is a typical process but we tailor this process to your specific needs so it may vary.

  • Learn More

But first, coffee

This step is the same for every client. We take the time to get to know you and your business. Not only does this make it easier for us to market your business but it also creates a relationship. Our goal in any client that we take on is to provide value first and foremost and we can't do that without trust.

Audit and Plan

The first step for any of our clients is to take a look at what has been done in the past. This is to get a better understanding of what we are working with. During this process, we will gather data and build strategies for how we plan to approach your digital marketing. We want to get a clear idea of what has worked for your business and what hasn't so we can build on the good and eliminate the bad. A big part of this process is the strategic planning aspect. During this phase, we will set the roadmap for success and come up with mutual goals.

  • Learn more

Build a Strong Foundation

After collecting all of the necessary data we will start getting to work. The most important part of any digital marketing is building a great foundation, just like a house. We will use this foundation to run testing and collect more data. The testing phase will begin as a part of the foundation step. During testing, we will test many different aspects of the digital marketing strategy: copy, images, landing pages, etc. This will help us build a strong understanding of how to scale our strategy down the road.

  • Learn more

Verify and Scale

Once we run the necessary testing we will begin verifying our benchmarks. We will be able to take the data we have collected to make informed decisions and establish target metrics and KPI's. Having this information will allow us to start the scaling process. At this stage, you will see the highest ROI/AS, and barring any unforeseen setbacks we should be able to scale exponentially.

  • Learn more

After the initial process

Once we begin the scaling phase we aren't done we are just getting started! When we can build one strategy that scales for your business it will become easier and easier for us to build another strategy. Think of it as a snowball effect. Your business is always changing and keeping up with the market and your marketing should reflect that.
