The 3-Step Journey

The 3-Step Journey is simple. It is the 50,000-foot view of the customer's journey. The journey begins with Awareness then moves to Consideration and finally conversion. We are here to help you along each step of this 3-Step Journey.

Awareness

The awareness stage is hyper-focused on the customer. It is all about providing useful content to customers. This can include anything from testimonials, tutorials, workshops, lessons, etc. The goal here is to provide a ton of value to your potential customers and build a relationship with them. The key to this step in the process is quality organic content.

  • Learn more

Consideration

In the consideration stage, we have enough data to go heavy into paid media and search ads. This stage is focused on the seven points of contact. The goal is to get in front of your customers as much as possible and remind them that you exist.

  • Learn more

Conversion

The conversion is the end goal. This stage is focused on where the digital transaction takes place. In most cases, this would be your website but it could also include third-party websites such as GrubHub, Doordash or Uber Eats for a restaurant. Conversions don't always mean sales, we are also referring to collecting qualified leads as conversions among many other types of conversions depending on your business. No matter how you measure conversions though, we help you by making sure the conversion process is as seamless as possible.

Can You Help Us With One Step?

Sure. We can. Would we recommend that? Absolutely not. You would still see results but not to the level of having the same team handling all three steps. We would recommend this even if you wanted to bring everything in-house. It is so important to have the same team handling every step along the process.
